What is the use of the Hoof Sinew

  • Eating restores 120 Fullness, but also causes Food Poisoning. Feed to pets to increase Fullness and EXP.

How to get Hoof Sinew

  • Go to the Action House to obtain it.
  • Defeat a strange beast to get Hoof Sinew.
