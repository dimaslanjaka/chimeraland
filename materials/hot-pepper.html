What is the use of the Hot Pepper

  • Eating restores 60 Fullness and grants Lv.1 Sandstorm resistance, but also inflicts cold. Feed to pets to increase Fullness and EXP.

How to get Hot Pepper

  • Gather in the Desert or at the Deserted Mountain to get Hot Pepper.
  • Orient the Resource Finder to find.
  • Go to the Auction House to obtain it.
