Freshwater Carp

October 6, 2022 10:26 PM
A freshwater fish with a slightly flattened body, a large head with a pointed end, and a flat and wide back of the head, with a raised dorsal posterior. The mouth is less prominent, and it has two pairs of whiskers, with a snout whiskers shorter than the jaw. Often found in Southmount freshwater.
