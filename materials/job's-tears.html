October 9, 2022 7:26 PM
Continue reading Job's Tears

What is the use of the Job's Tears

  • Eating restores 60 Fullness and reduces Humidity by 30, but also inflicts cold. Feed pets to increase Fullness and EXP.

How to get Job's Tears

  • Gather at the water's edge to get Job's Tears.
  • Orient the Resource Finder to find.
  • Auction House.
