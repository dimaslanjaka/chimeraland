What is the use of the Dung

  • A beast dung that is rich in nutrients and is used to fertilize. Reduces cultivation time by 30 minutes if used to fertilize plants, and grants 3000 Fertility if used to fertilize fields.

How to get Dung

  • Go to Auction House to obtain
  • Gather dung near the giant beasts to obtain
