What is the use of the Ice Piece

  • Eating restores 30 Fullness and grants Heat Resistance +5, but also inflicts cold. Feed to pets to increase Fullness and EXP.

How to get Ice Piece

  • Gather in the Tundra or at Snowy Peak to get Ice Piece.
  • Orient the Resource Finder to find.
  • Auction House.
