What is the use of the Chimeraland Beast Hide

  • It is an exclusive material used to create high-quality weapons and equipment. It can also be exchanged for the Beast Set in the Clan Shop.

How to get Chimeraland Beast Hide

  • Hunt and Search for any giant beast to obtain it
  • Got to the Auction House to obtain it.
  • Obtained from the Beast Hunt.
  • Orient the Resource Finder to find.
  • Explore the world to get.
  • Obtained from defeating giant beasts in Chimeraland.
