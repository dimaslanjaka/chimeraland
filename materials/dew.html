What is the use of the Dew

  • Consume to gain 20 Fullness and 5 Heat Resistance, but inflicts cold. It can be fed to pets to increase their Fullness and EXP, used to fertilize plants to reduce cultivation time by 5 minutes, or used to fertilize fields to increase Fertility by 500.

How to get Dew

  • Gather Wild Fruit to get Dew.
  • Explore world to get.
  • Resource Finder to find.
  • Auction House.
