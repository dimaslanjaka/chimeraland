What is the use of the Beast Hide

  • A usual item produced from searching strange beasts. Can be made into leather.

How to get Beast Hide

  • Go to the Auction House to obtain it.
  • Purchase Beast Hide from Grocer.
  • Purchase Beast Hide from Home Shop.
  • Purchase Newbie Resource Pack.
  • Purchase Prosperous Year (Small)
  • Defeat a strange beast to get this.
